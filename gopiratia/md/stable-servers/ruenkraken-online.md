**Battle Pass System A special system that allows you to get rare items by completing various quests**

- 
		Click on the special icon under the minimap
	

	- 
		48 reward levels available
	

	- 
		Depending on your VIP level, the reward may be better
	

	- 
		Complete daily tasks and gain experience
	

	- 
		In order to receive the main quests, you must finish main quests.
	

	**The system is under development and will be improved in the next update **

	 

	 

	 

	**Battle Pass**

	To open the battle pass you will have to click on the Battle Pass icon on the top right corner of your game.

	![image](https://kraken-online.org/forum/uploads/monthly_2023_12/536433774_-GoogleChrome2023-12-0818_30_59.png.88c32f201c46dca14aa1ab7aa859f05c.png)

	 

	**Battle Pass structure**

	**1. Progress**

	![image](https://kraken-online.org/forum/uploads/monthly_2023_12/1736018776_-GoogleChrome2023-12-0818_47_20.png.8be32d43367015f8404647fb62ead618.png)

	**What it consists of:**

	- Level and progress bar

	- Reward that can be claimed will be additionally shown

	- Player can claimed rewards depending on VIP level

	- 48 Battle Pass levels available

	 

	**2. Quests**

	![image](https://kraken-online.org/forum/uploads/monthly_2023_12/873931627_-GoogleChrome2023-12-0819_23_27.png.3bd5f9c04eaea3bcd85b59dd16abb637.png)

	**Note: 

	Gradation of opening quests - **Main quests completed -> "Collect" quests completed -> "Kill" quests completed -> 1200 Battle Pass Point

	This means that you will receive 1000 battle pass points only when you complete x2 Main quests, x5 Collect quests, x5 Kill quests

- Quests for collecting loot and killing monsters may vary
- Quests are not counted automatically! To complete the quest you need to click on the "Complete" button.
