Answer to question 1:

1. Go to your server files folder.
2. Go to Resources/script/calculate/variable.lua
3. Open it with a notepad.
4. You will see this:

EXP_RAID = 10 -- Experience rate
MF_RAID = 10 -- Fairy exp rate
Resource_RAID_ADJUST=1 -- Lifeskill drop rate
TeamExp_RAID = 20 -- Team experience

I hope i could help, good luck
