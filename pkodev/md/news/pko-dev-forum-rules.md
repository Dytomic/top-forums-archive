Hello everyone!

These are the important forum rules that you must comply with when using the forums. Breaking a rule here, may end up getting your forum account banned.

1. This is a civil forum, we will not be tolerating any hate posts towards other users.

2. Take your time and write the posts for what you want/look for, without rush-writing one.

3. Do not Spam post multiple posts, edit the existing one.

4. Do not Spam DM other people/and or be an annoyance towards other users.

5. No NSFW in this forum.

6. Messaging any Administrators asking for silly questions/help will not get you anywere, and is not allowed. Only message us if its important and/or if there is an issue with the forum.

7. Begging other users will not be tolerated.

8. Be polite when you write a post/or talk to people on the forum

9. Do not impersonate other users.

10. Spamming or flooding a post to boost your user post count will count towards spam and can result in account ban.

11. Account Selling/or Account Trading is strictly Prohibited

12. Multi Accounts/Dual accounts (multiple forum accounts) is not allowed, you are allowed to have one account and only one.

13. Any abuse of a feature or exploit on the website will not be tolerated.

14. Scamming of users will not be tolerated and you will not given a second chance.

Any of these rules may change at any time, so keep up to date with them here, and we will reply to this thread if there are any rule updates!
