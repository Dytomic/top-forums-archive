In This forum we are proud to have Various Roles that can be assigned to users such as: Server Owner (if you can prove you own and run a server) or Veteran (if you are a long lasting and trustworthy member from within the community for many years! (aswell as a well known name)

To request such a role, make sure to send me or any of the administrators a Private Message here on the forum, explain why you deserve it, and bring as much proof as you can to state and say that you are who you are. All these requests are for manual review.

Badges, much like Roles work the same way, if you have proof that you have made something, or are who you say you are, send me a private message here on the forum and it will be reviewd if you suit the criteria to get one.

Checkmarks, is the most rarest of them all, they are only assigned by admins (but not on demand) but if the admins recognize or see a pattern for a specific user that do fit to wear it in accordance of what it says it does. It is also required a verification (manually) after a staff member here on the forum think this particular member fits it, then he/she must be able to verify it on that user itself too.
