Tales of Pirates Forums Archive

Preserved snapshots of three community forums around the Tales of Pirates / Pirate King Online (PKO) game engine — captured for research and reference.

About this archive: These forums hosted years of community discussion, private-server development guides, mod releases, and engine reverse-engineering notes around the Tales of Pirates (TOP), Pirate King Online (PKO), and Kings of Pirates (KOP) engine family. They were scraped and archived here to preserve the technical knowledge, as community forums periodically go offline. Each forum is stored in both original HTML (unmodified) and Markdown (for easy search and reference).
